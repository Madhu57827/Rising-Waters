About Flood Prediction System

Using Artificial Intelligence to predict floods and improve disaster preparedness.

Our Mission

The Flood Prediction System is an AI-powered web application that predicts flood risks based on environmental and climatic conditions. It helps users understand potential flood threats before they occur, supporting early preparedness and decision-making.

Our goal is to combine Machine Learning with an easy-to-use interface to provide accurate and timely flood predictions.
